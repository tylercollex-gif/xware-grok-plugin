# Privacy (user-facing)

XWare continuous improvement is designed so **your game stays yours**.

## What we aim for

- Learning stays **on your machine** by default (local hub).
- Shared signals are **aggregate quality metrics only** — not your save games, not your meshes, not your art files.
- Cloud sharing is **off** unless you deliberately turn it on.
- Elevates and installs are built to **avoid destroying player saves**.

## What marketing will not do

Public posts and marketplace blurbs talk about **games results and graphics**, not internal safety mechanics.

## Operators

Runtime safety lives with the Godot install / SOT tools. Rehydrate with `install_xhance.ps1` after CLI updates.
